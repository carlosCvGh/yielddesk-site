YieldDesk — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

YieldDesk ("the App") is a real estate deal analyzer and property management app developed by Carlos Vidaurre ("the Developer"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data Collection

YieldDesk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter into the App — including property details, financial projections, tenant information, and expense records — is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud, synced privately through your personal Apple iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

If you choose to use iCloud, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is accessible only to you. The Developer does not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud privacy practices are governed by Apple's own privacy policy, available at apple.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

YieldDesk offers in-app purchases and subscriptions processed entirely through Apple's App Store. The Developer does not collect or have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple. For details, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Sign in with Apple

YieldDesk uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. The Developer receives only the information you choose to share during sign-in (such as your name and email address, which may be a private relay email). This information is used solely for account identification within the App and is not shared with third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

YieldDesk does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. The App does not contain ads. The App does not track your activity or share data with advertisers.

Third-Party Services

YieldDesk does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services beyond Apple's own iCloud and App Store services.

Data Retention and Deletion

All your data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. You can delete your data at any time by deleting individual records within the App or by deleting the App entirely. Removing the App from your device removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud data, you can manage your iCloud storage through your device's Settings.

Children's Privacy

YieldDesk is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
