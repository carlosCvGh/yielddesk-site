Frequently Asked Questions

Is YieldDesk free?

The Analyze tab is free with your first 3 projections included. Unlimited projections are available as a one-time purchase of $9.99. The Manage and Portfolio tabs require a subscription at $4.99/month or $29.99/year, which includes a 14-day free trial.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any previous purchases or active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes. YieldDesk uses iCloud to sync your data across all your Apple devices automatically. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device's Settings.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export any deal analysis as a PDF or CSV file to share with partners, agents, or lenders.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to your device's Settings, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, and select YieldDesk to manage or cancel your subscription.
